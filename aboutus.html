Who We Are

Universal Steganography Tool is a privacy-focused web application that lets users hide messages within text, images, audio, and video entirely on the client side. We believe in giving people full control over their sensitive communications: all operations happen locally in your browser, with end-to-end encryption and no data ever transmitted to our servers.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower individuals and organizations with an easy-to-use, robust steganography solution for secure, private communication.

Vision: A world where privacy tools are universally accessible and seamless, allowing everyone to communicate safely without fear of interception.

Key Features

  • Hide messages in text, images, audio, and video.
  • 100% client-side processing: no server storage or transmission of your data.
  • End-to-end encryption: your hidden messages are encrypted in-browser before embedding.
  • Simple, intuitive interface: protect your privacy with just a few clicks.
  • Cross-platform: works on modern browsers across desktop and mobile.

How It Works

  1. Choose Carrier Type: Select whether you want to hide your message in text, an image file, an audio file, or a video file.
  2. Enter or Upload: Provide the carrier (e.g., paste the cover text, or upload an image/audio/video file) entirely in your browser.
  3. Encrypt & Embed: Type or paste your secret message; the tool encrypts it end-to-end, then embeds it into the carrier using steganographic algorithms.
  4. Download Locally: Receive the modified carrier file (or transformed text) for you to store or share. No data ever leaves your device.
  5. Extract & Decrypt: On the recipient side, they load the stego-carrier in their browser, enter the correct passphrase/key, and the hidden message is decrypted and revealed locally.

All cryptographic operations and steganography embedding/extraction happen entirely in your browser using proven client-side libraries. This ensures maximum privacy: we never see or store your secret messages.

Security & Privacy

We take security seriously. Everything runs locally:

  • Client-Side Only: No network requests for your message or carrier—everything happens in-browser.
  • End-to-End Encryption: Messages are encrypted before embedding; only someone with the correct passphrase/key can decrypt.
  • No Data Retention: We do not collect, log, or store any of your data on our servers.
  • Open Philosophy: We encourage you to inspect the code (if open-sourced) or test with sample files to verify that nothing is transmitted externally.

Note: While steganography adds a layer of secrecy, always use strong passphrases and follow best practices for key management. Use responsibly and ethically.
